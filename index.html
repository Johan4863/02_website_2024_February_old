Projects

Anime TCG Bot

Anime Card Collector Bot is a simple yet enjoyable Discord bot that allows you to collect cards featuring your favorite anime characters. You'll find characters like Kafka from Honkai Star Rail, Saitama from One Punch Man, or even Almir, one of the creators of the game Payday. The bot lets you collect cards, trade them, and send them on various missions. Each card has its own unique abilities and elements that you can utilize in the game. Despite its simplicity, Anime Card Collector Bot provides the pleasure of collecting and trading cards, while fostering a friendly community of anime enthusiasts. Join us and explore the world of anime through the lens of a card!
