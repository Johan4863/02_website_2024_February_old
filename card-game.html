About

This website is dedicated to showcasing the Card Game Discord Bot project. The bot is designed for playing a card game and collecting anime cards within Discord servers.

Features

  • Play a card game within Discord.
  • Collect anime cards featuring your favorite characters.
  • Customize gameplay settings and rules.
  • Compete with friends for high scores.
